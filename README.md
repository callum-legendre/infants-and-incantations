# Infants and Incantations

### An RPG with real time combat, a spell slinging baby, and a corrupt bureaucracy.
---
## Overview

Infants and Incantations is a metroidvania RPG where the player takes the role of a baby wizard who is attempting to escape daycare.
Players will ascend through the daycare and its bureaucracy, rescuing other babies and uncovering corrupt buisness practices with a customizable loudout of spells and equipment.

**Core Concept:**
- *Customisable Loudout:* Players will be able to discover new spells and equipment to develop their own playstyle and solve problems in different ways.
- *Manipulatable Environment:* Players will be able to use spells and other tools to manipulate the daycare environemnt to their advantage.
- *Quest system:* Players will be able to complete quests assigned to them by other baby NPCs.
- *Open ended:* Players can solve problems however they would like, and have free roam of the world.
